Our Mission & Story

We're a student-led club dedicated to exploring STEM through hands-on projects, collaboration, and innovation. Formed by a group of passionate students, our club was built to inspire curiosity and build real-world skills outside the classroom.

What We Do

Our club meets regularly to learn new technologies, build creative projects, host workshops, and work together as a team. We participate in hackathons, mentor each other, and share our skills in coding, electronics, and design.
